1. Purpose

These Terms and Conditions govern all services offered by Clean Cars Wash (AHMED-YAHIA LM, IDE CHE377.481.423), specialising in premium mobile car washing and detailing across the cantons of Geneva and Vaud, Switzerland. Placing a booking constitutes full and unreserved acceptance of these Terms.

2. Services

Clean Cars Wash offers the following detailing packages:

  • Silver Pack — CHF 89.- (full interior & exterior wash)
  • Gold Pack — CHF 149.- (Silver + polish + wax protection)
  • Diamond Pack — CHF 289.- (full detailing, leather treatment & ceramic coating)

Monthly subscription plans and custom business quotes are also available under specific terms (see section 6).

3. Booking & Deposit

All bookings are confirmed upon payment of a 30% deposit of the total service price. The deposit can be paid online via TWINT or credit/debit card (Visa / Mastercard). The booking is only definitively confirmed upon receipt of the deposit payment. The remaining balance (70%) is collected directly by the technician upon vehicle delivery.

4. Cancellation & Delays

  • Any client delay of more than 15 minutes without prior notice may result in cancellation of the booking and forfeiture of the deposit.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service are non-refundable; the deposit is retained by Clean Cars Wash as compensation.
  • Cancellations notified more than 24 hours in advance allow the full deposit to be credited to a future booking at no charge.
  • Clean Cars Wash reserves the right to cancel or reschedule an appointment in the event of force majeure (extreme weather, technical unavailability, etc.) without financial penalty.

5. Vehicle Condition

  • The client must remove all personal belongings, documents, and valuables from the vehicle before handover to Clean Cars Wash.
  • Clean Cars Wash accepts no liability for valuables, documents, accessories, or equipment left inside the vehicle's cabin or boot.
  • If the vehicle is found to be excessively dirty (heavy pet hair, thick mud, significant organic contamination), a surcharge of CHF 69.- per hour will be assessed and communicated to the client before work begins. The client may accept or decline the surcharge; in case of refusal, the service is cancelled and the deposit is retained.
  • Pet hair removal is a dedicated add-on service charged at CHF 39.90.

6. Subscriptions & Business Quotes

Monthly subscription plans (Essential, Serenity, Premium) and corporate solutions are subject to a specific commitment defined at the time of signing the personalised agreement. Pricing is established by quotation based on vehicle size and service frequency. Early termination must be notified in writing with a minimum notice period of 30 days.

7. Liability

Clean Cars Wash is committed to performing all services with care and professionalism. Any quality complaint must be submitted within 48 hours of vehicle delivery by email to info@cleancarswash.ch or by telephone at +41 78 265 70 71. No claims will be accepted beyond this period.

8. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

These Terms and Conditions are governed by Swiss law. In the event of a dispute concerning their interpretation or performance, the parties agree to seek an amicable resolution. Failing this, any dispute shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the ordinary courts of the Canton of Geneva.
