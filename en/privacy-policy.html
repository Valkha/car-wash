Privacy Policy

Last updated on April 5, 2026

1. Personal Data Collection

When using our site, particularly through our contact and quote request forms, we collect the following data:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Vehicle Information (Make, Model, Condition)

2. Purpose of Collection

The collected data is strictly necessary to:

  • Prepare a personalized quote for automotive detailing services.
  • Contact you to schedule an appointment.
  • Manage the commercial follow-up of your request.

Under no circumstances will your data be sold, transferred, or leased to third parties for commercial marketing purposes.

3. Transmission and Security

To ensure our contact forms function correctly, the entered data is securely transmitted via the third-party API Web3Forms before being sent to us by email. We implement all reasonable measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your information.

4. Retention Period

Your personal data is kept for a maximum duration of three (3) years from our last contact, in accordance with current regulations (GDPR).

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, port, or delete your data, or limit its processing.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at any time at the following address:

contact@car-wash.com
